## Rules

These are non-negotiable:
- No em-dash character
- No exclamation marks anywhere
- Subject line under 50 characters, no clickbait
- Body between 50-150 words
- Never use: "I hope this email finds you", "I wanted to reach out", "I came across your profile", "synergy", "circle back", "low-hanging fruit", "just following up"
- Do NOT put the company name in the subject line
- Must include at least one question (engagement hook)
- Must reference at least one specific detail from the prospect's profile, posts, or experience
- First name only, never "Dear Ms./Mr."

Write the subject line and body. Make it feel like a real person wrote it for this specific person.
